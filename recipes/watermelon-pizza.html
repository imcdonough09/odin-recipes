Grilled Watermelon Feta Pizza

Description

This grilled watermelon feta pizza is your showstopper for the summer. Grill a thick slice of watermelon, top with a quick-pickled onion, feta, and walnuts for a watermelon salad like you've never had.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 red onion thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil, divided
  • 1 (1 1/2-inch thick) slice watermelon, from a large seedless watermelon
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped toasted walnuts
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly cracked black pepper
